## Summary

A phone webshop on vimla.se where customers can browse the latest iPhones and Samsung Galaxy phones, and purchase them on 36-month instalment plans bundled with a Vimla subscription. The goal is to increase ARPU, improve new-customer conversion, and reduce churn by removing the need to buy devices elsewhere.
